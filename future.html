Word Fleet

Each phase represents a major strategic advancement in Word Fleet capabilities. Deployment schedules are classified, but these features are currently in various stages of tactical preparation and field testing.

Phase Alpha - Simulator Mode

In Planning

Train your fleet in virtual combat scenarios with AI-powered battle simulations and tactical analysis. Perfect your strategy against computer opponents before facing human adversaries.

Features

  • AI opponents with adjustable difficulty
  • Battle replay and analysis tools
  • Training scenarios and challenges
  • Performance statistics tracking

Phase Bravo - Tournament Mode

In Planning

Command your fleet in organized competitions with leaderboards, brackets, and championship glory. Rise through the ranks to become Fleet Admiral.

Features

  • Seasonal tournaments and leagues
  • Global leaderboards and rankings
  • Achievement badges and titles
  • Spectator mode for major battles

Phase Charlie - Mobile App Combat

In Development

Deploy your word-ships on-the-go with native mobile applications for iOS and Android devices. Battle anywhere, anytime with touch-optimized controls.

Features

  • Native iOS and Android apps
  • Cross-platform battle compatibility
  • Push notifications for turns
  • Offline play capabilities

Phase Delta - SMS Battle System

Future

Engage in naval warfare through text messages - no internet required for remote battle coordination. Perfect for commanding fleets in remote locations.

Features

  • Text-based battle commands
  • No internet connection required
  • Automated game state tracking
  • SMS-friendly battle notation

Phase Echo - AI Captain Challenges

Future

Face off against artificial intelligence commanders with adaptive strategies and varying difficulty levels. Each AI captain has unique tactical preferences.

Features

  • Multiple AI personality types
  • Adaptive difficulty scaling
  • Unique captain backstories
  • Progressive challenge campaigns

Phase Foxtrot - Online Multiplayer

Future

Connect with captains worldwide for real-time naval battles and strategic fleet management. Build alliances and wage tactical campaigns.

Features

  • Real-time multiplayer battles
  • Global matchmaking system
  • Friend lists and private matches
  • Cross-platform compatibility

Phase Golf - Ranked Battles

Future

Climb the naval hierarchy through competitive ranked matches with seasonal rewards and titles. Prove your tactical superiority on the global stage.

Features

  • Skill-based matchmaking
  • Seasonal rank resets
  • Exclusive ranked rewards
  • Detailed match analytics

Phase Hotel - Campaign Mode

Future

Lead your fleet through epic story-driven campaigns across dangerous waters and enemy territories. Uncover the mysteries of the Word Fleet universe.

Features

  • Story-driven single-player campaigns
  • Branching narrative choices
  • Special mission objectives
  • Unlockable fleet upgrades
