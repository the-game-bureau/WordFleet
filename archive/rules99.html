A Mini-Game to Determine First Strike

Each Captain has 5 secret Launch Codes (000â€“999). Use them to bluff your way to firing first!

  1. Opening Hail: One Captain calls out any 3-digit code.
  2. Response: The opponent must choose: Concede, Challenge, or Counter.
  3. Continue: Players alternate until someone concedes or is challenged.

Engage the Enemy

Initial Strike

Select a coordinate and announce it: "E-7"

MISS
No ship at coordinate. Turn ends.
HIT
Ship confirmed! Proceed to Letter Strike.

Letter Strike

Guess a letter at the coordinate: "S"

BULLSEYE
Correct letter. Turn ends.
TALLY
Wrong letter. Total count revealed. Turn ends.
Defender: Track hits and update your manifest accordingly.

Victory

Destroy all five enemy Word-Ships to win the game.

Advanced Protocols

Sinking Enemy Vessels

Reveal all letters to sink a ship. Announce: "You sank my [WORD]!"

Vessel Destruction Protocol

Instead of firing, guess the entire word.

  • Correct: Vessel destroyed instantly
  • Incorrect: Turn ends and word is off-limits forever
