01

Your Mission

Locate and destroy enemy word-ships hidden across the attack grid. Use logic, deduction, and word knowledge to outmaneuver your opponent in this fusion of naval warfare and word puzzles.

02

Mission Specs

Personnel: 2 captains

Minimum Age: 10

Mission Duration: 15-30 Minutes

03

Requisition

  • 2 Battle Tracker sheets from https://the-game-bureau.github.io/wordfleet
  • 2 Pens or Pencils
  • 2 Highlighters (optional)
04

Fleet Deployment

Deploy five word-ships on your Defense Grid. Use either auto-generated or custom words; both players must use the same method.

word-ship Classification Length Example
KETCH5 lettersPIANO
SHIP4 lettersJUMP
SUB3 lettersBED
ARK3 lettersRUN
PT2 lettersIF
  • word-ship classifications are for reference only and do not affect gameplay
  • Place word-ships horizontally (left-to-right) or vertically (top-to-bottom)
  • No diagonals, backwards, or upward placements
  • Ships may touch but not overlap
  • No proper nouns, abbreviations, or suffixes
05

Hailing the Enemy

Determine who strikes first through a mini-game of strategy and deception.

Both captains have 5 launch codes (000–999) stamped on their battle tracker. Use codes, strategy and deception to try and secure the first strike!

1
Opening Hail
Call out a launch code, real or fake
2
Response
Respond with one of 3 choices:
Concede – Give up
→ Original captain strikes first
Challenge – Demand proof
→ Code is valid: original captain strikes first
→ Code is fake: challenging captain strikes first
Counter – Call an equal or higher code
→ Now the original captain must respond to this new code
3
Continue
Continue until someone gives up or is caught lying
Sample skirmish
Original captain: "850" → Other captain: "900" → Original captain: "Challenge!"

Result: Other captain reveals no 900 launch code → Original captain strikes first
06

Engage the Enemy

With first strike determined, battle begins! Choose one of two tactical options each turn.

On your turn, choose one of two tactical options:

  • Standard Attack – Initial Strike on a coordinate, with potential Alpha Strike follow-up if you hit a word-ship
  • Demand Surrender – Attempt total victory by revealing the enemy's entire fleet

Standard Attack

1
Initial Strike
Choose a coordinate and call it out:
"Fire on coordinate Echo-Seven!" or "E-7!"
Responses:
MISS No ship. Mark with a dot. End turn.
"Miss!"
HIT Ship confirmed. Highlight the coordinate's borders. Proceed to Alpha Strike.
"Hit."
2
Alpha Strike
Guess the letter at the coordinate:
"Alpha Strike Sierra!" or "S."
Responses:
BULLSEYE Correct letter at that position. Write it in. End turn.
"Bullseye."
LETTER TALLY Wrong letter. Announce how many total times that letter appears in your fleet. End turn. Attack this same coordinate again later to try different letters.
"S tally 3."

Demand Surrender

1
Decisive Strike
At the beginning of any turn (instead of taking that turn), demand surrender by stating every word-ship and its exact location. Wait until all word-ships are announced before responding:
"I demand your surrender! Your fleet consists of: PIANO at Charlie-3 through Charlie-7, JUMP at Alpha-1 through Alpha-4, BED at Echo-8 through Echo-10, RUN at Bravo-5 through Delta-5, and IF at Foxtrot-2 through Foxtrot-3!"
Responses:
YOU HAVE WON Every word and location is correct. Achieve total victory.
"You have won."
VICTORY IS MINE! YOU LOSE! GOOD DAY SIR! Anything is wrong (word, spelling, or coordinates). Immediately surrender and lose the game.
"Victory is mine! You lose! Good day sir!"
07

Defense Operations

Track your opponent's attacks and respond accurately.

When being attacked:

  • Mark dots for enemy misses on Defense Grid
  • Highlight borders for confirmed hits on Defense Grid
  • Trace over each letter discovered via bullseye on Defense Grid

Important: When all coordinates of a word-ship are hit, announce which word-ship has been revealed. Do NOT announce the word itself.

08

Themed Battles

For variety, deploy fleets around specific themes:

  • Biological: ZEBRA, FROG, COW, BAT, OX
  • Directional: NORTH, LEFT, TOP, WAY, UP
  • Additional themes: Musical, Celestial, Culinary, Oceanic, Metallic, Geometric, Temporal, Athletic, Botanical, Atmospheric
09

Difficulty Adjustment Protocol

For uneven battles, use Manual Selection to deploy custom-chosen high-difficulty words while your opponent uses Auto-Generated word-ships.
