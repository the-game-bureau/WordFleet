Your Mission 🎯

Your mission is to locate and destroy enemy word-ships hidden across the attack grid. Use logic, deduction, and word knowledge to outmaneuver your opponent in this fusion of naval warfare and word puzzles.

Mission Specs 🗺️

Personnel: 2 Captains

Minimum Age: 10

Mission Duration: 15-30 Minutes

Requisition 📊

2 Battle Tracker sheets from https://the-game-bureau.github.io/WordFleet

2 Pens or Pencils

2 Highlighters (optional)

Fleet Deployment ⚓

Each captain deploys five Word-Ships on their Defense Grid. Use either auto-generated or custom words; both players must use the same method.

Word-Ship Classification Length Example
KETCH5 lettersPIANO
SHIP4 lettersJUMP
SUB3 lettersBED
ARK3 lettersRUN
PT2 lettersIF
  • Word-Ship classifications are for reference only and do not affect gameplay.
  • Place Word-Ships horizontally (left-to-right) or vertically (top-to-bottom)
  • No diagonals, backwards, or upward placements
  • Ships may touch but not overlap
  • No proper nouns, abbreviations, or suffixes

HAILING THE ENEMY 📡

A mini-game to decide who strikes first

Both captains have 5 launch codes (000–999) stamped on their battle tracker. The codes, strategy and deception are all used to try and secure the first strike!

1
Opening Hail
Either captain calls out a launch code, real or fake
2
Response
The other captain has 3 choices:
Concede – The other captain gives up
→ The original captain strikes first
Challenge – The other captain demands proof
→ Code is valid: original captain strikes first
→ Code is fake: challenging captain strikes first
Counter – The other captain calls an equal or higher code
→ Now the original captain must respond to this new code
3
Continue
Repeat until someone gives up or is caught lying
Sample skirmish
Original captain: "850" → Other captain: "900" → Original captain: "Challenge!"

Result: Other captain reveals no 900 launch code → Original captain strikes first

Engage the Enemy ⚔️

Cry havoc and let slip the dogs of war! (Too much?)

On your turn you can: 1) Standard Attack -Announce a coordinate to locate and identify hidden word-ships 2) Demand Surrender

Standard Attack

1
Initial Strike
Choose a coordinate and call it out:
"Fire on coordinate Echo-Seven!" or "E-7!"
Responses:
MISS No ship. Mark it with a dot. Turn ends.
HIT Ship confirmed. Proceed to Letter Strike.
2
Letter Strike
Guess the letter at the coordinate:
"S"
Responses:
BULLSEYE Correct letter at that position. Write it in. Turn ends.
TALLY [LETTER] {COUNT} Wrong letter. Defender announces how many total times that letter appears in their fleet. Turn ends.

Demand Surrender

1
Bold Declaration
At the beginning of any turn (instead of taking that turn), demand the enemy's surrender by stating every word-ship and its exact location:
"I demand your surrender! Your fleet consists of: PIANO at Charlie-3 through Charlie-7, JUMP at Alpha-1 through Alpha-4, BED at Echo-8 through Echo-10, RUN at Bravo-5 through Delta-5, and IF at Foxtrot-2 through Foxtrot-3!"
If every word and location is correct, you achieve total victory If anything is wrong (word, spelling, or coordinates), you immediately surrender and lose the game

Defense Operations 🛡️

When you're being attacked:

Mark dots for enemy misses on Defense Grid

Highlight borders for confirmed hits on Defense Grid

Trace over each letter that has been discovered via bullseye on Defense Grid

Themed Battles 🦓

Biological: ZEBRA, FROG, COW, BAT, OX

Nutritional: PASTA, MILK, PIE, TEA, EGG

Environmental: CLOUD, TREE, SUN, SKY, ICE

Asymmetric Warfare Protocol

One operator deploys custom-selected high-difficulty words while opponent uses auto-generated standard designations.
