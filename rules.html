Your Mission

Your mission is to locate and destroy enemy word-ships hidden across the grid. Use logic, deduction, and word knowledge to outmaneuver your opponent in this fusion of naval warfare and word puzzles.

Mission Specs

Personnel: 2 Captains

Minimum Age: 10

Mission Duration: 15-30 Minutes

Requisition

2 Battle Tracker sheets from https://the-game-bureau.github.io/WordFleet

2 Pens or Pencils

2 Highlighters (optional)

Fleet Deployment

Each captain deploys five Word-Ships on their Defense Grid. Use either auto-generated or custom words; both players must use the same method.

Word-Ship Classification Length Example

  • Word-Ship classifications are for reference only and do not affect gameplay.
  • Place Word-Ships horizontally (left-to-right) or vertically (top-to-bottom)
  • No diagonals, backwards, or upward placements
  • Ships may touch but not overlap
  • No proper nouns, abbreviations, or suffixes

Engage the Enemy

Initial Strike

Select a coordinate and announce it: "E-7"

MISS
No ship at coordinate. Turn ends.
HIT
Ship confirmed! Proceed to Letter Strike.

Letter Strike

Guess a letter at the coordinate: "S"

BULLSEYE
Correct letter. Turn ends.
TALLY
Wrong letter. Total count revealed. Turn ends.
Defender: Track hits and update your manifest accordingly.

Victory

Achieve victory by completely identifying and destroying all five enemy Word Ships through precise letter detection and word reconstruction.

Defense Operations

Mark dots for enemy misses on Defense Grid

Highlight perimeters for confirmed hits on Defense Grid

Cross out destroyed vessels upon enemy word identification
