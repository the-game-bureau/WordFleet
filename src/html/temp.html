Vector vs Raster Graphics

Vector Graphics
Made of mathematical paths and shapes
Infinitely scalable without quality loss
Small file sizes for simple designs
Best for: Logos, icons, simple illustrations
Raster Graphics
Made of individual pixels
Quality depends on resolution
Larger file sizes for detailed images
Best for: Photographs, complex artwork
