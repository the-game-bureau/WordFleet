Rules of Engagement

Master these tactical principles to dominate the high seas of word combat. Victory belongs to those who understand both strategy and vocabulary.

Fleet Deployment

  1. Each fleet contains 5 wordships of different sizes
  2. Ships can be placed horizontally or vertically
  3. No ships may touch each other
  4. All vessels must fit within the 10x10 grid
  5. Record ship positions on your defense grid

Combat Operations

  1. Players alternate calling out grid coordinates
  2. Announce "HIT" or "MISS" for each attack
  3. Mark all shots on your attack grid
  4. When a ship is sunk, announce the vessel type
  5. First to sink all enemy ships wins the engagement

Wordship Classifications

  1. KETCH: 5-letter heavy cruiser
  2. SHIP: 4-letter battle cruiser
  3. SUB: 3-letter submarine
  4. ARK: 3-letter support vessel
  5. PT: 2-letter patrol boat

Advanced Tactics

  1. Use systematic search patterns for efficiency
  2. Employ probability to predict ship locations
  3. Vary your own deployment strategies
  4. Master psychological warfare techniques
  5. Adapt tactics based on opponent behavior
